The objective of this experiment is to get more familiar with KVL and KCL and superposition.

$ V_{a}=5V \,\,\,\,\,\, V_{b}=10V \,\,\,\,\,\, R_{1}= 22k\Omega\,\,\,\,\,\, R_{2}=33k\Omega \,\,\,\,\,\, R_{3}= 10k\Omega $

\end{flushleft}
\begin{flalign*}
& V_{2}+V_{1}=V_{A} \\
& V_{2}+V_{3}=V_{B} \\
& i_{1}+i_{3}=i_{2} 
\end{flalign*}
\begin{flushleft}

\end{flushleft}

\begin{flalign*}
